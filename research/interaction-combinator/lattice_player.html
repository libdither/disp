RUNG 2 REPLAY — THE LIFTED LATTICE, FRAME BY FRAME

A replay client over rust-ca-lattice traces (the engine is the model; this page only draws). Cells live at (x,y,z): bilayer traces have two planes (the 2.5D chip), full3d traces as many as the run used — upper planes draw offset up-right and dimmer. There are no vias and no tucks: a crossing is two wires at different z. Each tick: ψ heat and tension-survey sweeps, then fire every enabled adjacent facing pair (blocked pairs evict one strand from their room — the fire's right of way), slide one blocking strand out of each stuck walker's way (the excluded-volume move), reel every ψ-demanded producer one cell (eating its own aux drag where it can), flip bends one cell toward annihilating partners the survey found (tension: cold wires tighten everywhere at once; demanded wires at a deliberate trickle, so contraction never fights the walk), shove parked agents one cell down the χ gradient — routing-free: the agent moves only when every wire re-ties through its own footprint, by eating its own chain or one bend — while pressure also hops cold bends downhill and evaporates crowded wire (the field moves wire cell by cell, no search), then retract U-turn slack to fixpoint — the only step that destroys wire length, fed by the flips. Deterministic: the same term always produces this exact movie. Long traces are stride-thinned (fire frames always kept, events merged). Regenerate or add traces with cargo run --release --bin dump-run <name> [ticks] [bilayer|full3d] [stride] and drop the JSON here.

AGENTS & PORTS

Port 0 is always the principal (▸): producers emit their value there, consumers consume there, and a fire needs the two principals facing across one edge. Aux ports carry everything else. In the canvas the principal is the thick white stub.

WHAT THIS SHOWS (AND DOES NOT)

Correctness is schedule-independent by construction: every transition declares its cell footprint (writes machine-checked inside it) and preserves the projection onto the shadow interaction net. Liveness is measured, never assumed. The schedule carries three strictly neighbor-local signals: ψ demand (the gold hot bit, one cell per tick; it replaced the unbounded wire trace as reel's gate), the tension survey (each strand learns which directions its wire travels beyond each side; bends flip toward annihilating partners and retract cashes them out, so wires finish near chord length — the chain4 trace used to freeze holding 774 strands and now completes holding 5), and χ pressure (Jacobi-relaxed, pumped only by real frustration; it shoves parked agents, evaporates wire mats, and fences tension out of contested rooms). Every embedded full3d trace reaches NF; the bilayer presets left STUCK on purpose show the no-basement residue. The corpus residue is LOCAL_CA_DESIGN.md §13's remaining liveness question.
