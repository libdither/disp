RUNG 1 — FIELD ON A WIRE-ONLY LATTICE

Von Neumann 4-neighbor. Nodes are inert (no firing). Wires are chains of forwarder cells; two can cross as a via (purple). Everything relaxes under one field: tension (shorten wires) + pressure (make room). The tick is a synchronous Margolus-style commit: all nodes propose from the same frozen state, a conflict-free subset commits by rotating priority. Tests the two riskiest unknowns before any reduction: the jamming threshold and the liveness watchdog.
