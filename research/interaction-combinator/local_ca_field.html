RUNG 2 — IC EVALUATION ON A LOCAL LATTICE

A tree-calculus interaction net reduced on a von Neumann lattice. Agents (values L S F P, consumers A T₁ T₂ δ ε N) sit on cells; links are forwarder chains. Every wire edit is local (an agent step reels/bends each wire by one cell, no router); values reel in and fire only when adjacent, and erasers reel in to drain garbage gradually. Reduction is demand-driven; the result is delivered to the fixed OUT pad (☉). Checked live against an independent normalizer (the differential oracle).
